What to expect

What happens when you visit.

01

We prepare for you

Before your visit we'll have the pianos you're interested in tuned, positioned and ready to play. Your time here is focused entirely on finding the right instrument.

02

Play at your own pace

Whether you're a beginner or an advanced player, take as long as you need. We're here to answer questions, not to sell you.

03

Ask us anything

From the history of each piano to its restoration, regulation and touch — we'll walk you through everything you want to know.

04

Leave with confidence

Whether you buy on the day or need time to think, you'll leave knowing exactly what you're considering.
