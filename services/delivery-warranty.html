The tuning truth

Most piano shops don't tell you this.

Every piano goes out of tune after being moved — it's completely normal. The change in environment, humidity and temperature affects the strings. Most shops leave you to figure this out yourself. We don't. A certified tuner is automatically booked for 3–4 weeks after your delivery, completely free, so your piano sounds perfect from day one.
